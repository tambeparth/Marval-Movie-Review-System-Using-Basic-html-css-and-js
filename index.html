AVENGERS: ENDGAME
Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 superhero film produced by Marvel Studios and directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. It is the culmination of over a decade of storytelling in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). In the aftermath of Avengers: Infinity War, where the villainous Thanos wiped out half of all life in the universe, the surviving Avengers embark on a final, desperate mission to reverse the devastating effects of the Decimation and restore order to the universe. The film features an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy Renner, Don Cheadle, Paul Rudd, Brie Larson, Karen Gillan, Danai Gurira, Benedict Wong, Jon Favreau, Bradley Cooper, and Josh Brolin. Avengers: Endgame delivers an epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga, tying up loose ends and providing satisfying closure for many beloved characters while setting the stage for the future of the MCU.

CAPTAIN AMERICA
Captain America: Civil War is a 2016 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Captain America, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, the film features an ensemble cast including Chris Evans as Captain America, Robert Downey Jr. as Iron Man, and many other characters from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The plot revolves around a disagreement between Captain America and Iron Man over government oversight of the Avengers, leading to a rift between the superheroes and culminating in an epic battle between two factions of the Avengers.

DR. STRANGE
Dr. Stephen Strange is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by artist Steve Ditko and writer Stan Lee, Dr. Strange first appeared in "Strange Tales" #110 in 1963. Originally a talented but arrogant neurosurgeon, Dr. Stephen Strange's life takes a dramatic turn after a car accident severely damages his hands, ending his surgical career. Desperate to regain his former skills, Strange embarks on a journey that leads him to the Ancient One, a powerful sorcerer in the Himalayas. Under the Ancient One's tutelage, Strange learns the mystic arts and becomes the Sorcerer Supreme, Earth's primary protector against mystical and magical threats. Dr. Strange's adventures often involve him defending the Earth and the multiverse from supernatural menaces while grappling with his own inner demons and moral dilemmas.

GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXY
Guardians of the Galaxy is a 2014 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. Directed by James Gunn, the film stars Chris Pratt, Zoe Saldana, Dave Bautista, Vin Diesel, and Bradley Cooper as the titular Guardians.
